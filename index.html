about

Welcome to the Music-Automaton!

This application was created with the purpose of providing inspiration material for musicians to help them find ideas for their songs by generating automated 16 measures melodies and chords from your inputs. Once your random melody is created, you can then download the MusicXML file (readable by music notation softwares such as Musescore) or share the direct link on social media platforms.

We hope this will help inspire you to create more original music!
